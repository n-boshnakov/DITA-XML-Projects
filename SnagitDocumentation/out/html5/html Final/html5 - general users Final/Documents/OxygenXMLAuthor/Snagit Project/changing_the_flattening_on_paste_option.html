Changing the Flattening on Paste Option

You can modify the way pasting an element behaves in Snagit.

You have installed Snagit on your computer.

When you paste an image in Snagit, by default it places it on the file, but allows you to modify it. This grants you a greater degree of control over your screenshot. If you want to have an image be immediately flattened when pasted, you can do so by chaging the options for it.

By repeating these steps, you can revert to your previous selection.

  1. Open Snagit Editor.
  2. Navigate to Edit > Editor Preferences....

  3. Navigate to Advanced.
  4. Choose Automatically flatten pasted images.

  5. Choose OK.

After completing these steps you will have changed your current pasting settings.
