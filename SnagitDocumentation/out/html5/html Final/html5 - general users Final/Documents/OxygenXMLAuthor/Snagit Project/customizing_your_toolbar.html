Customizing your Toolbar

You can swap out and reorder the tools in your toolbar.

You have installed Snagit on your computer.

When you work with Snagit, you might want to add or removed certain tools from your toolbar, or reorder your currently selected tools. You can do so in a few steps by customizing your toolbar.

  1. Open Snagit Editor.
  2. Navigate to More > Customize Toolbar.

  3. Right click on the tool you want to add to the toolbar.
  4. Select Add to toolbar.

  5. Optional: Drag and drop your tools to reorder them.
  6. Select Done when finished.

After completing these step the tools you selected will be added to your toolbar.
