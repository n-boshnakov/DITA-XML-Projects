Remapping your Print Screen Button

You can change the button used for printing your screen.

You have installed Snagit on your computer.

You can choose which button prints your screen. By default Snagit does not associate any button with this function.
  1. Open Snagit.
  2. Choose Add Hotkey.

  3. Press the key you want to map.
After completing these steps you will have remapped the button Snagit uses to print your screen.
